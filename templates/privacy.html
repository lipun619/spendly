Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your email address and hashed password, used to identify your account.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly submit.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and generate spending summaries.
  • To authenticate you when you sign in.
  • To diagnose errors and improve the reliability of the Service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply standard security measures — encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls — to protect your information.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — font assets are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log requests per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at support@spendly.app.

For details on your rights and obligations when using the Service, see our Terms and Conditions.
